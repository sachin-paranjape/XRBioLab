Laboratory Manual & Controls

Desktop & Mobile Controls

  • Look Around: Left-click & drag (or swipe on screen) to look around the lab.
  • Move & Pan: Right-click & drag (or use keyboard arrow keys) to move inside the laboratory space.
  • Inspect Organs: Left-click or tap on any glowing hotspot sphere to select it, spawn its display model, and play the audio explanation guide.
  • Deselect Model: Left-click on the floor or background to close the active display model and stop the audio guide.
  • Toggle Manual: Press the 'A' key on your keyboard or click the "CONTROLS MANUAL" button at the top-right corner to show/hide this manual anytime.
